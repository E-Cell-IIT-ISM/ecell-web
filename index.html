Frequently Asked Questions

Know about us a little more

What are the startups currently present in E-Cell?

E-Cell has one currently running startup and another in its pre-launch phase! The co-founders of these startups are the final year members of team E-cell.

What about any startup related events taking place in IIT (ISM) Dhanbad?

Creating a startup friendly atmosphere, ecell hosts events like the bizathon which includes a workshop as well as product building and pitching competition. Also, an E-TALK is held where influential speakers and entrepreneurs incubate the startup atmosphere.

Can one request for a mentor in bizathon(event held in concetto)?

Bizathon is an event conducted by ecell during concetto(institute’s techfest) includes a workshop of business management and an overnight product hackathon where the participants have to mould their idea in a business model and pitch the same to the judges in shark tank. Here, mentors will be provided for both the overnight and pitching events and ecell team members will always be accessible if any aid is required!

Do startups have an edge over corporate job or the reverse is true?

Actually direct comparison between startups and corporate jobs is not feasible. Both simply are two different ideologies. If you have an idea and want to create your own firm then you can clearly opt for startups. Whereas if you prefer financial security then you can always opt for a corporate job. Your skills of managing risks again becomes a deciding factor!

MBA before entrepreneurship: myth or compulsion?

Truly saying none! However going for MBA would make you acquainted with the business scenario but again as we know entrepreneurs are not businessmen truly! Also, having subjects like investment management or public finance would make you have an edge over the others in the market. But one can always have people hired for these jobs to be done! Since entrepreneurship is all about identifying problems and solving them, you become an entrepreneur at the instant you identify a problem and start working towards finding an effective solution to it!
