GUIDELINES

  • Students must fill all the information correctly. In case of any false information, they will be barred from applying in DIF.
  • E-Cell does not guarantee an assured internship.
  • Students are supposed to provide valid proofs of all the points mentioned in the resume afterwards to the company. In case of any false/invalid information, the candidate will be blacklisted from DIF and respective company policy will be applied thereafter.
  • Companies will shortlist the candidates and E-Cell has no authority to intervene in the process.
  • Shortlisted students will be directly contacted by the company for their preferred way of evaluating the students(telephonic/skype interview)
  • Applicants cannot contact the HR/person associated with the internship profile. All the communication will be through E-Cell.
  • InCase of any dispute, the decision of E-Cell IIT(ISM) Dhanbad will be final.
